1. Introduction

Welcome to the UJ Stabus System. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, student number, and login credentials.
  • Location Data: Real-time location of buses and student geolocation (with permission).
  • Usage Data: App interactions, booking history, and preferences.
  • Emergency & Maintenance Data: Details related to emergency alerts or maintenance logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real-time bus tracking and notifications.
  • To manage bookings and bus capacity.
  • To improve system performance and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates or emergencies.
  • To generate anonymous statistical data for operational improvements.

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be shared with third parties, except when:

  • Required by law or university policy.
  • Necessary for emergency services.
  • Required by internal university departments for service optimization.

5. Data Security

We use secure servers and data encryption protocols to protect your information. Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

6. User Rights

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request data deletion where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for geolocation tracking.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through the app and official university channels.

About UJ Stabus

UJ Stabus is a real-time transport service designed to help students easily travel between the University of Johannesburg's four campuses: Doornfontein, Auckland Park Bunting, Auckland Park Kingsway and Soweto Campus. We offer live bus tracking, seat reservations, and weather updates to ensure a smooth, efficient, and comfortable journey for all students.

Our Mission & Values

  • Reliability: Providing accurate, live updates on bus locations and ETAs to minimize waiting time.
  • Transparency: Offering real-time tracking and clear communication between students and transport operators.
  • Safety: Ensuring the safety of passengers during their commute by keeping everyone informed.
  • Efficiency: Maximizing the on-time arrivals and minimizing wait times.

How It Works

  1. Real-time GPS data allows students to track bus locations and estimated arrival times.
  2. Students can reserve seats in advance, ensuring a smoother, more organized boarding process.
  3. Stay updated on weather conditions to plan your journey effectively and comfortably.
  4. Receive notifications about bus delays, seat availability, and weather-related changes.
